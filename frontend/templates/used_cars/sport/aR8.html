Description

The 2025 Audi R8 represents the pinnacle of Audi’s performance engineering — a perfect blend of precision, power, and design. Featuring a naturally aspirated V10 engine, rear- or all-wheel-drive configurations, and lightweight construction, the R8 delivers an exhilarating driving experience both on the track and on the road. Its sharp styling, luxurious interior, and advanced technology make it a true supercar icon.

Key features

  • 5.2-liter naturally aspirated V10 engine with breathtaking performance.
  • Available in rear-wheel drive (RWD) or quattro all-wheel drive.
  • Luxurious interior with Nappa leather, virtual cockpit, and sport seats.
  • Advanced Audi Virtual Cockpit with performance display modes.
  • Lightweight aluminum and carbon-fiber construction for superior agility.

Description

The 2025 Porsche 911 Carrera continues the legendary legacy of the 911 lineup with a perfect balance of performance, precision, and everyday usability. Its unmistakable design and rear-engine layout deliver a thrilling driving experience, while the interior offers modern luxury and cutting-edge technology. Built for purists and enthusiasts alike, the 911 Carrera remains one of the most iconic sports cars in the world.

Key features

  • Iconic 3.0-liter twin-turbo flat-six engine delivering exceptional performance.
  • Rear-engine, rear-wheel-drive layout for authentic 911 driving dynamics.
  • Porsche Communication Management (PCM) system with high-resolution touchscreen.
  • High-quality interior with sports seats, digital displays, and customizable trims.
  • Optional Sport Chrono Package for enhanced driving performance and response.

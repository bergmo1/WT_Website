Description

The 2025 Chevrolet Corvette Stingray embodies the spirit of the American sports car, featuring a mid-engine layout that delivers exceptional handling, balance, and performance. Its bold design, driver-focused cockpit, and powerful V8 engine make it one of the most capable and accessible high-performance cars on the market. With cutting-edge technology and unmistakable style, the Corvette Stingray offers pure driving excitement in every detail.

Key features

  • 6.2-liter LT2 naturally aspirated V8 engine mounted in a mid-engine configuration.
  • 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission for lightning-fast gear shifts.
  • Driver-oriented cockpit with premium materials and digital instrument cluster.
  • Available Z51 Performance Package for enhanced handling and cooling.
  • Removable roof panel for open-top driving experience.

Toyota RAV4 Hybrid

Images

Description

The 2025 Toyota RAV4 Hybrid is a reliable and efficient compact SUV that combines a petrol engine with an electric motor for smoother performance and lower fuel consumption. It offers a comfortable ride, modern design, and a spacious interior suitable for both city driving and long trips. The hybrid system delivers strong power while maintaining excellent efficiency, supported by Toyota’s reputation for durability and advanced safety technology.

Key features

  • Hybrid powertrain combining petrol engine and electric motor for high efficiency and smooth driving.

  • Available all-wheel drive (AWD) for improved traction and stability on various road conditions.

  • Spacious and comfortable interior with modern infotainment and driver-assistance systems.

  • Strong reliability, low fuel consumption (~5.6 L/100 km), and reduced emissions compared to petrol-only SUVs.

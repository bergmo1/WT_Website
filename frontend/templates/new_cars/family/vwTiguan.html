Volkswagen Tiguan

Images

Description

The 2025 Tiguan marks the third generation of Volkswagen’s best-selling SUV — redesigned from the ground up for greater efficiency, comfort, and technology. It introduces new 48-volt mild-hybrid engines and plug-in hybrid (PHEV) options with up to 100 km of electric range. Inside, it features a fully digital cockpit, a large central infotainment display, and high-quality materials that elevate the cabin feel. The exterior adopts a bolder, more aerodynamic design with improved airflow and sharper lines.

Key features

  • The third generation of the Tiguan has been completely redesigned: styling, interior, chassis.
  • New digital cockpit and infotainment system: large touch-screens, more connectivity.
  • Improved aerodynamics: drag coefficient improved from ~0.33 to ~0.28.
  • A broad range of powertrains: mild-hybrid (eTSI), TSI petrol, TDI diesel, and from 2024/25 plug-in hybrid (eHybrid).

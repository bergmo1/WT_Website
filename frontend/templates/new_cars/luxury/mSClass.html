Description

The 2025 Mercedes-Benz S-Class represents the pinnacle of luxury, innovation, and performance. Designed to deliver the ultimate driving experience, it features state-of-the-art comfort systems, next-generation driver assistance, and refined craftsmanship throughout the cabin. With its elegant design, cutting-edge infotainment, and smooth hybrid powertrains, the S-Class remains the benchmark for premium sedans worldwide.

Key features

  • Luxurious interior with premium leather, ambient lighting, and advanced comfort seats.
  • MBUX infotainment system with 3D instrument cluster and large OLED touchscreen display.
  • Rear-wheel steering for improved agility and ride comfort.
  • Advanced driver assistance systems for semi-autonomous driving and safety.
  • Available plug-in hybrid (S 580 e) with up to 100 km of electric range.

Description

The 2025 BMW 7 Series redefines modern luxury with a perfect blend of innovation, comfort, and performance. Designed for those who expect the very best, it features an expressive exterior design, state-of-the-art technology, and first-class comfort in every seat. Its cutting-edge infotainment system, advanced driver assistance, and choice of powerful engines — including plug-in hybrid and fully electric options — make the 7 Series a true flagship of the BMW lineup.

Key features

  • Executive lounge interior with massaging seats, ambient lighting, and premium materials.
  • BMW Curved Display and iDrive 8.5 infotainment system with natural voice control.
  • Optional 31.3-inch 8K Theater Screen for rear passengers.
  • Adaptive air suspension and integral active steering for supreme comfort and agility.
  • Available as plug-in hybrid (750e xDrive) or fully electric (i7) models.
